Guidelines for Farmers:

  1. Step 1: Upload Pest Image
    Click on the "Choose an image" button to select a photo of the pest affecting your crops. Ensure that the image clearly shows the pest.
  2. Step 2: Submit Your Image
    After selecting the image, click on the "Submit" button to upload it to our system. Please wait patiently while the image is being processed.
  3. Step 3: Pest Identification and Analysis
    Once the image is uploaded, our system will analyze it to identify the pest species and provide detailed information.
  4. Step 4: Pest Management Recommendations
    Based on the identified pest and its lifecycle stage, you will receive personalized pest management recommendations.
